The Manual Data Entry Challenge

In today's fast-paced trucking industry, lead capture often follows this painful process:

  1. Manual Photo Review: Sales teams manually review truck photos to identify USDOT numbers

  3. Manual Research: Copy and paste carrier information from SAFER web lookups
  4. Manual CRM Entry: Transfer all collected data into Salesforce or other CRM systems
  5. Manual Follow-up: Track engagement status manually across multiple platforms
